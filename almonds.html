Ingredients

  • 2 cups unsalted roasted Almonds
  • 2 tbsp. Maple Syrup
  • 1 tbsp. chopped fresh Rosemary
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. Vegetable Oil

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350.
  • In bowl, mix all ingredients.
  • Scrape onto parchment-lined baking sheet.
  • Bake, stir once, until golden and toasted, about 15 minutes.
Makes 2 cups.

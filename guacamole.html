Ingredients

  • 3 Avocados-cubed
  • 3 tbsp. fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 Mango-cubed
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped Red Onion
  • 1 Chipotle Chile in Adobo Sauce, seeded and minced
  • 2 tbsp. chopped Cilantro.

Instructions

  • In bowl, mash avacados with lime juice.
  • Stir in mango, onion, chipotle, and cilantro: season.
Serves 8

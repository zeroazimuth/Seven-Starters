Ingredients

  • 3 tsp. ground Cumin
  • 2 1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
  • 2 tsp. ground Coriander
  • 1/2 tsp. Cayenne salt & pepper
  • 3 lbs. chicken wings
  • 2 tbsp. EVOO
  • 1 1/2 cups Greek Yogurt
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped Cucumber
  • 1/2 cup chopped Cilantro

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 450.
  • In small bowl, mix cumin, garlic powder, coriander, cayenne and 1 tsp. salt.
  • In large bowl, toss wings with EVOO.
  • Sprinkle with spice mixture: toss and coat.
  • Arrange in single layer on rack set over baking sheet; season.
  • Bake, flipping once until golden, 40 minutes.
  • Meanwhile in another small bowl, mix yogurt, cucumber and cilantro; season.
Serve with Wings. Serves 8.
